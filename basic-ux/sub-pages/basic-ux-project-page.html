Thoughts on the theme Basic UX

During the design sprint we were able to work as a team to create something that I honestly think would be worth pursuing. We went through so many different steps in such a short week, but it was the most engaged and motivated I have felt during this semester. My role as the facilitator was something that my team asked me to do, but it is something that I am actually really good at and was hoping to do. Theme Goal:

To use the Google design sprint method to create a native app idea to improve student life for KEA students.

Takeaway:

During the design sprint we were asked to adapt to a fast-paced environment where decisions needed to be made quickly, but with thought put in to them. We all had to be functioning at our highest. Working with a team all day for five days was a new experience, but I was lucky to have a great team. We needed to understand all of the steps of the design sprint while simultaneously getting comfortable with creating and testing prototypes.
